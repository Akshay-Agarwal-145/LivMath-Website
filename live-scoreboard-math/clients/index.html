Welcome to the LivMath competition!

LivMath is a math competition, which mimics the structure of a competitive programming competition, like codeforces. Each problem will have a different point weightage, depending on the speculated difficulty of the particular question. Your team's goal is to solve the problems as quickly as possible. The point weightage of each question gradually decreases, so you have to make sure to solve them fast! There will be a live scoreboard with every team's scores in real time, so that you can see how to win! The best teams will be getting high cash prizes!

For more information regarding the specific details of the contest, feel free to go to the about the contest page.

Regarding prize money, refer to the about the contest page as well as the sponsors page for the necessary information.

For other questions, please go to the FAQ page. If your question is not addressed there, then submit your question on the contact page, and the FAQ will be updated.

For all additional questions, please email livmath.faq@gmail.com
